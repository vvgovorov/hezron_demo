Bio

Hezron received his Bachelor of Science degree in City and Regional Planning from California Polytechnic University, San Luis Obispo, in 1994 and his Juris Doctor degree from Indiana University School of Law in 1997. Hezron is also a graduate of the Higher Ambition Leadership Institute.

Hezron was raised in Los Angeles, and moved to the Midwest following his schooling where he attributes the growth and maturity of his personal values. He received his J.D. from Indiana University School of Law (Bloomington), then moved to Wisconsin where he spent the first 15 years of his career leading M+A efforts for multiple global organizations. Currently, Hezron lives in Pheonix, Arizona with his wife and children. In his time away from the office, Hezron is a proud father and tennis coach to his children, two of whom are ranked among top athletes in the country.

Always looking to expand his knowledge, Hezron is an avid reader and utilizes the help of a librarian to curate his reading switching topics of focus every six months. His current area of study is focused on the history of income and wealth inequality. Putting his ongoing education to work, Hezron is also a published writer, having five papers appear in peer reviewed journals. Hezronâ€™s writing covers global mergers + acquisitions, brand licensing, and negotiation.

Hezron currently serves on the Editorial Board of the Association of Corporate Counsel, and previously served on the Board of Directors of Northwestern Mutual Capital and the China Business Counsel of the Milwaukee Metropolitan Association of Commerce.

Leadership / Management style

  • Set high, but achievable standards, while being sincerely empathetic.

  • Style ensures that the corporate vision, and any specific vision set for the legal department, articulated and provide adequate direction to team members.

  • Establish trust and commitment, leading to the generation of new ideas and achievement of high standards of excellence.

  • Primary Leadership Style: Affiliative and Authoritative; Myers Briggs: ENTJ.

  • Provide long-term professional development within an environment that is supportive and harmonious.

  • Team development approach is to align team member talents and aspirations with the strategy and direction of the company.

  • Focus on enhancing talent by providing support, direction and opportunities for group and individual success.
